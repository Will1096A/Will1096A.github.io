Rhino Facts

  • There are only five species of Rhinos across the world which are:
  • White rhinos mainly in South Africa with populations in East Africa.
  • Black rhinos all across East and Southern Africa.
  • Indian rhinos in just India and Nepal.
  • Javan rhinos which are critically endangered on the Indonesian island of Java.
  • Sumatran rhinos which live on the Indonesian islands of Sumatra and Borneo, that are also few in number.
